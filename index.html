Renewable Energy

Electricity is one of the biggest industries in the modern-day. We use electricity in everything we do, from entertainment to refrigeration. Electricity is primarily powered through hot air turning a generator, which converts mechanical energy to electrical energy. To heat the air, however, we need to burn a heat source.

Fossil fuels, while being the leading source of this type of energy for the United States, are harming the environment and climate more each day. Carbon footprints and fossil fuels are increasing rapidly, which is harmful to the environment. Renewable energy sources can be used in place of harmful energy sources. These renewable energy sources naturally replenish themselves, meaning there is an abundance of them on Earth. Using renewable energy sources will have a major positive impact on the environment. These resources not only help the environment, but it also creates a diversity of resources for others to use. As governments expand more into renewable resources and they become cheaper for the consumer, it is vital for families to invest in renewable resources.

Our Purpose

We created this website allow others to learn more about the environment and the harm we bring upon it when we are not careful with the resources we use. Our Information page provides information on 5 different types of energy sources. We also created the survey for others to be more aware of the best renewable energy source they can use that's near them. We hope to have a positive impact on everyone, and for everyone to be inspired to use resources that are better for their environment.

Who is R.E.G.I.E?

R.E.G.I.E stands for renewable energy guidance intelligence engineering. It is a virtual intelligence bot who helps find the most easily accesible energy source for the user. By analysing the data from specific questions regarding geography and household income, R.E.G.I.E is able to determine if solar energy, geothermal energy, hydroelectric energy, wind energy, or bioenergy is the best suited renewable energy source.
